# Ease & Aura — Marketing Checklist

_Last updated: 2026-07-14_

The whole business is blocked on one thing: **traffic → affiliate clicks.**
No clicks means Sovrn stays "pending review" and Amazon's 3-sales-in-180-days
clock runs out. Everything below exists to fix that one gap.

Honest expectations before you start:
- **There is no fast free traffic** for a new affiliate site. Pinterest is
  weeks; SEO is months. Anyone promising faster is selling something.
- **Don't run paid ads.** Amazon pays 3–4% commission — you'd spend more on the
  click than you'd ever earn. Not for this business.
- **Skip Instagram for now.** A new IG account posts into the void. Pinterest is
  a *search engine*, so a new account can still get seen. That's the difference.

---

## Priority 1 — Pinterest (the fastest lever you have)

Fashion + capsule wardrobes is one of Pinterest's strongest categories, and pins
rank on the search query, not on your follower count.

- [ ] Create a **Pinterest Business account** (free) for Ease & Aura
- [ ] Set the profile: name "Ease & Aura", bio mentioning "capsule wardrobe /
      free style quiz", link to easeandaura.com
- [ ] **Claim your website** (Settings → Claimed accounts). This unlocks
      analytics and boosts your pins' distribution — important, don't skip
- [ ] Create 5 boards: _Capsule Wardrobe_, _Wardrobe Essentials_,
      _Fall/Winter Style_, _What to Buy_, _Style Over 40_ (or your target)
- [ ] For **each of the 4 guides**, make 2–3 pin images (so ~10 pins total)
      - Vertical, 1000×1500px
      - Text overlay = the search someone types: "The Best Cream Sweater,"
        "How to Buy Jeans That Last," etc.
      - Link each pin to that guide's URL (e.g. easeandaura.com/guides/best-camel-coat)
- [ ] Also make 2–3 pins for the **quiz itself**, linked to /app.html
- [ ] Pin **2–3 per day**, not all at once — Pinterest rewards steady posting
- [ ] After 2 weeks, check Pinterest analytics: which pins get impressions?
      Make more like the winners.

> brand (Cormorant + Jost, cream/gold) — just ask.

## Priority 2 — Get the guides found on Google (the durable asset)

This is the real long-term business. Slow, but it compounds — pages that rank
earn commissions on autopilot for years.

- [ ] Set up **Google Search Console** for easeandaura.com (free)
- [ ] Submit the sitemap: `https://easeandaura.com/sitemap.xml`
- [ ] Confirm the 4 guide URLs are indexed (Search Console → URL Inspection)
- [ ] Confirm the site is **not blocking crawlers** — check robots.txt isn't
      set to `noindex` (you had a noindex issue on ReLaunch Her; verify here)
- [ ] Over the next few weeks, watch which guides get impressions and for what
      queries — that tells you which guide to write next
- [ ] **Write one new guide per week** while the others age. Next candidates:
      trench coat, ballet flats, white button-down, black trousers, leather tote

- [ ] **Finish the Amazon Associates tax interview.** Payout is blocked until

      "you clicking in the Amazon portal" job; I can walk you through it.)
- [ ] Confirm the **Amazon tag** is `easeandaura20-20` everywhere (already fixed

      automatically. Nothing to do but drive traffic.

## What NOT to do (so you don't waste effort)

- ❌ Don't build the curated SKU catalog yet — it's gated on Amazon PA-API,
      which needs 3 sales first. Wrong order.
- ❌ Don't re-debug the Sovrn integration. It's verified working. It's a
      traffic problem, not a code problem.
- ❌ Don't spin up Instagram/TikTok for traffic. Fine for credibility later,
      useless for clicks now.
- ❌ Don't pay for ads.
